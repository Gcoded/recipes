Ingredients:
 - dough
 - tomato sauce
 - shredded cheese
 - pepperoni


Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

 2. Roll dough into a flat circle.

 3. Spread tomato sauce around the dough.

 4. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of tomato sauce.

 5. Add pepporonis and heat in the oven for about 30 mins.
